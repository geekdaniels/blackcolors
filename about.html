About Us

Blackcolourgroup is the brand name of Blackcolour Aesthetics Nig. Ltd, a company that has since 2004 been dutifully involved into the business of Art, Furniture, Interior Design, Doors, Soundproofing and Building Construction. The company’s experience, knowledge and professional skill have resulted in making of beautiful artworks with high compositional value; manufacturing of elegant and durable furniture in various ranges and creating of awesome interior designs categorized to suit clients’ taste and budget. With the concept of design build employed in building construction our projects are mostly executed from conception to completion necessitating the working together of various skilful architects, engineers and designers giving rise to spectacular edifices with painstaking finishing!

Our use of high quality renewable materials, latest machines and software, contemporary technological solutions and skilful professionals in the industry has made productivity and service delivery beat targets in the face of competitions.

The company`s edge over seeming competitors is the setup of a one-stop-shop for art, furniture, interior design, doors, soundproofing and building construction - interrelated and inter-dependent business units. They form a formidable synergy strengthening the activities of the company and at same time enabling clients to get what they really want within their budgets!
