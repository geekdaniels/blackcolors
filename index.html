INTERIOR DESIGN

From concept to completion interior design is a fundamental aspect of our projects. We use modern computer aided design solutions to develop interiors before commencement of project execution. The approach helps in avoiding unwanted structural and aesthetic designs leaving as a result a desirable and bespoke setting. We have provide three interior design options suitable to client’s taste and budget – these are Basic, Cozy and Full Options! Residential, homes, offices, hotels, factory structures, auditorium, estates and public buildings are our major project center of attention.

Basic Option

This is a standard interior setting with basic decorative items and furniture. A client of considerable budget could use it to set up service apartments, standard living spaces, holiday homes, etc.

Cozy Option

Cozy option targets are office spaces, hotel rooms and middle class living. It provides you with all necessary comfort and drives away feeling of stress associated with office task and nostalgia accompanying one in a hotel away from home.

Full Option

It is a resolution of maximum comfort with employment of all elements of interior design and decoration, at their best. It goes after clients with impeccable taste, reasonable budget and custom design intent.
